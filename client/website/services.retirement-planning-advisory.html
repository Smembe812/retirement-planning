Retirement Planning Advisory

This is comprehensive programme targeting all the employees of your organisation, at every level and grade and age. This programme is fully practical and offered through our Customer Service Portal. Participants are guided to examine themselves and think through independently of the life style in retirement. Once that life style is determined, participants are guided in finding solutions on how to raising and saving and investing the money for retirement. During this programme participants will assess their net worthy position (Personal Balance Sheet) and devise an investment plan as well restructure their debt position as planning for retirement (Investment Strategy). This programme takes recognisance of those participants who are at the point of retirement. They are counselled on what to do next in retirement and take intelligent investment decisions. They are made aware of the available resources to them. It is a programme full of financial planning, wealth management, debt management and counselling where crisis looms high and life and survival is bleak
