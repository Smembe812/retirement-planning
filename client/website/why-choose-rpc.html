National Network Local Experts

We are the first and only national provider for retirement planning design, consulting, and administration services for individuals. With 100+ experts located across the country, RPC delivers local services to its 1000+ clients, backed by the strength of its national resources—people, technology and operational protocol.

Our Advantage

We are a neither Pension Fund Managers/administrators nor Asset Managers, meaning we partner with our financial intermediaries on client engagements and don’t compete with them for investment or insurance sales.

Our Expertise

Our primary resource is our people. Our national team of 100+ credentialed experts (consultants, financial planners and advisors, Attorneys, etc.) works together to ensure our clients are maximizing retirement savings opportunities and minimizing taxes. Our people present at industry conferences and have been published in national trade journals. We host numerous informational webinars for clients and financial intermediaries on industry hot topics, plan design, etc. and offer client updates though our quarterly newsletters.

The Scope of Our Services

Our extensive collection of expertise allows us to offer a wide range of retirement planning services, investment and wealth management services from basic to sophisticated plan design, through annual compliance and administration services for any plan size, to general consulting services.

Our Commitment to Maximizing Value

Our consultants work with our clients and advisors to provide retirement plan solutions that maximize value. Whether our clients are looking for a complex plan design or set of plans to serve a very specific purpose, or a more simple straightforward approach, we work to design and implement asolution that is best suited to meet each client’s goals and objectives in the most cost-effective and tax efficient manner

Our Experience

We have the experience necessary to provide our services accurately and efficiently.
