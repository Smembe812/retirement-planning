Training programmes for individuals.

Our team of experts run public or generic and inhouse courses in the areas of Retirement Planning, Investments, including gall our general counselling. All our Public courses are announced on this Website under News. These Public courses will run once per quarter in Lilongwe and Blantyre. You must register and pay online for a place to be reserved for you. Once you have paid online, you will be notified that a place has been reserved for you and an account created in your name in the Customer Service Portal. You will immediately start receiving news alerts through the social media platforms once you have registered. Inhouse programmes will involve organisations looking for a tailor-made programme for their specific cadre of staff or entire organisation. In cases where, organisations with staff members not less that 100, and the organisation intends to train all staff, executives only would be exempted from course fees. Inhouse programmes require a proposal and specific contracts. Please call our proposals line +265999978453 immediately and you will be assisted.
