About Us

We are the first Malawian Retirement Planning Centre founded and managed by local Malawians to assist fellow Malawians plan for descent life in retirement. Our vision is Malawi of people who have retired at will and by design with well crafted Retirement Plans. We are a group of professional financial planners, counsellors and advisors. We are appalled by the current state of affairs that people breakdown and cry loud once news is broken to them that its time to retire. We are in front of the battle of fighting deaths which occur due to unplanned retirements. We have designed our centre around you.

National Network Local Experts

We are the first and only national provider for retirement planning design, consulting, and administration services for individuals. With 100+ experts located across the country, RPC delivers local services to its 1000+ clients, backed by the strength of its national resources—people, technology and operational protocol.
