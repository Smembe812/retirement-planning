Counselling

Our experience is that currently majority of our valued customers have not planned for retirement and they are proceeding on retirement shortly. They are in a serious crisis that the annuities bought from the Pension fund cannot financially support their desired life style let alone cost of maintaining dependents. Others have gone to extent of committing suicide. Due to this situation, they develop so many healthy related complications including hypertension and heart problems. What they may need more is counselling. If you are or your retiring employees are in that state, we are just a call a way. Please call us and arrange for a counselling session and explore some coping strategies relevant for and unique to your or situation.
